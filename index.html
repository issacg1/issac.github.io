Why Coding?

My love for coding stems from my experience as CEO of ZippySpot and Founder of ShiraLi. Being in a start-up environment required me to play several roles, including assisting the development team. Having no prior education in coding, I turned to YouTube & Udemy to get my feet wet. I started out by creating some simple HTML and CSS sites and loved it. I decided to further my education and started to learn JavaScript. Throughout my unofficial education, it boggled me to understand what databases were and how they were created and accessed from the browser. After ZippySpot was privately acquired, I had the opportunity to pursue a proper education in web development. Loving what I learned so far, pushed me to take the plunge and Join GAs Full Stack Web Development boot camp to get a formal and proper coding education.
